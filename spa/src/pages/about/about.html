About

Sensor hub 1.0

Sensor hub is a small tool used as to illustrate the process of data aquisition in a network of sensors and a myriad of devices connected altogether to form a communication platform. This tool is based on the technology which is known as Internet of Things (IoT) and focuses rather demonstrates on how Machine-to-Machine (M2M) communication is achieved.

This tool is a companion to the Sensor hub device unit and designed such a way to emulated or at leat mimic the appearance or workflow of the device itself.
